Industry Profiles

Business Analyst

Talks to the customer to figure out what they actually want.

Talks to developers to figure out what can actually be done

Software Developer

Writes the actual code

Tester

Works with the Developers and BAs to decide how requirements will be tested

Runs the tests as developers deliver things and reports on their success

Deployment/Platforms Engineering

Manages the environments into which systems are going to be deployed

Provision infrastructure, write deployment scripts.
